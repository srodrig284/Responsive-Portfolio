About Me

I graduated from Pace University in 1983 with a Bachelor of Business Administration in Management Information Systems. Back then it was very tough to get an entry level position in IT. So I pursued a career as a Realtor. Real Estate sales was convenient and allowed me the opportunity to raise my daughters and have a flexbile schedule.


Finally, in 1999 I got my first programming position with Worldspan. Worldspan was a Global Distribution System for the Airlines. After a few years, Worldspan was acquired by Travelport. In 2005, Travelport close the South Florida office and I was laid off.


Later that year, I was hired as a contractor by EDS as a programmer for their airline area. I language called Prolog. It's a very interesting and fun language, somewhat similar to C but with a twist.


In 2007, Travelport hire me back as a contractor working remotely. In 2014, Delta Airlines acquired the Deltamatic System from Travelport and took the employees and contractors. In 2014, I was converted to a contractor for Delta Airlines.


I've been working from home for 9 years now. I love it! My goal is to work as a freelance Web developer or get hired by a good company as an employee. I am going to own this Web Developer Bootcamp!!!
